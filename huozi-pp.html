This Privacy Policy explains how COMPANY_NAME ("we", "our", or "us") handles information when you use APP_NAME (the "App").

1. Overview

APP_NAME is a Chinese learning app with HSK vocabulary, grammar, radicals, sounds, handwriting sheets, mini-games, and phrasebooks. We do not require you to create an account, and we do not ask you to provide your name, phone number, or email address in order to use the App.

2. Information Stored on Your Device

The App stores certain data locally on your device so core features can work properly. This may include:

  • learning progress, XP, levels, streaks, and review status;
  • selected language, theme, and app preferences;
  • daily reminder settings;
  • saved folders, pinned items, and custom word collections;
  • handwriting sheets, tags, page settings, and exported sheet metadata;
  • cached learning resources and character stroke data.

This local data is primarily stored on-device. If your device backups are enabled through Apple, some app data may also be included in your device backup according to Apple's settings.

3. Analytics

In release versions of the App, we use AppMetrica to understand how the App is used and to improve stability and product quality.

Analytics may include events and technical information such as:

  • app opens and screen usage;
  • language setting and visit counts;
  • interactions with reminder prompts and similar in-app flows;
  • device type, operating system version, app version, and other technical diagnostics;
  • identifiers and technical data made available by the platform or analytics SDK.

We do not use analytics to identify you by name. However, third-party analytics providers may process technical information under their own privacy terms.

4. Character Stroke Data and Network Requests

The App includes local stroke-order assets for many Chinese characters. If a character is not available locally, the App may request character stroke data from a third-party CDN hosting Hanzi Writer data.

When this happens, the provider may receive technical request data such as your IP address, request metadata, and the specific character file requested.

5. Notifications

If you enable daily reminders, the App uses Apple's local notification framework to schedule reminders on your device. These reminders are created locally and do not require you to create an account.

You can disable notifications at any time in the App or in your device settings.

6. Exporting and Sharing

The App allows you to export handwriting sheets and share them using the iOS share sheet. When you choose to share exported content, the selected destination app or service will handle that content according to its own privacy practices.

We do not upload your handwriting sheets or exported files to our own servers as part of normal app use.

We use information processed by the App to:

  • show reminders if you enable them;
  • improve app performance, usability, and reliability;
  • maintain cached resources for faster loading.

We do not sell your personal information.
